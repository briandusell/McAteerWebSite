"Share With Jesus His Lenten Journey to Calvary"

A Lenten spiritual retreat

Marytown Retreat and Conference Center
1600 West Park Avenue
Libertyville, IL 60048
The week of March 24-28, 2014

Experience Newfound Freedom and Personal Development
Through the Spiritual Exercises of St. Ignatius Loyola

This midweek retreat is intended for those interested in developing their spiritual life. It wil begin at 7:00 p.m. on Monday, March 24 and conclude at 1:00 p.m. on Friday, March 28. In this directed/guided retreat, the number of participants will be limited to eight (8). We will have two presentations each day with mass and homily reflection. Participants will be encouraged to meet with me every other day. The prayer content will be to reflect on the major point in St. Ignatius Loyola's Spiritual Exercises with complimentary Bible texts.

To aid a sense of group community, retreatants are requested to reside at Marytown during the retreat. Failing to reach the desired number of eight residents, commuter retreatants will be accepted to complete the remainder.

Registration Information

Please register soon. A limited number of places are available for this retreat weekend.
The cost is $220.00 per person for retreatants who will be staying at the Marytown Center and includes the cost of rooms and meals.
The cost would be $75.00 for commuter retreatants.
All rooms at Marytown are double occupancy.

About Fr. McAteer

Fr. Patrick McAteer, SJ is engaged in sacramental ministry, spiritual counseling, and retreats at St. Ignatius parish, Chicago, IL. Since 1975, he has given the Spiritual Exercises to religious and lay men and women. He has helped the spiritually wounded through his healing program.
